# Welcome to Bookmark Board!

This extension transforms your cluttered Chrome bookmarks bar into a highly visual workspace. By replacing the standard list view with an intuitive, drag-and-drop layout, your bookmarks finally feel like a personalized, interactive mood board.

---

## Why Bookmark Board?

Standard bookmarks are easily forgotten in hidden dropdown menus. Bookmark Board brings your saved pages front and center, organizing nested folders into clean, manageable blocks so you can find exactly what you need at a single glance.

## Core Features

* **Visual Board Layout:** See your bookmarks laid out visually with their native favicons for instant recognition.
* **Drag & Drop Organization:** Effortlessly reorder bookmarks or folders and arrange your workspace exactly how you want it.
* **Easy Edits via Context Menu:** Right-click on the board to create a new folder, add a fresh bookmark, rename, or delete old items without having to open Chrome's default manager.
* **Switchable Themes:** Instantly change the vibe of your workspace with a variety of built-in themes.
* **Quick Search:** Instantly filter through your bookmarks and folders to find that one elusive link.
* **Fully Compatible with Native Manager:** Any changes you make in Chrome's built-in Bookmark Manager will be reflected on your board automatically.
