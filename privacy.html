Privacy Policy for Bookmark Board

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

Bookmark Board respects your privacy. This policy describes how the extension handles data:

1. Data Collection

Bookmark Board does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data to external servers. All your bookmark data is processed locally on your machine using Chrome's official APIs.

2. Use of Permissions

  • Bookmarks: Used only to display and organize your bookmarks on the board.
  • Storage: Used to save your local preferences, such as selected themes.
  • Favicon: Used to display website icons for easy recognition.

3. Third Parties

Since no data is collected, no data is ever shared or sold to third parties.
