## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-06

### Added

- Visual Board Layout: Initial release featuring a visual workspace for Chrome bookmarks.
- Drag & Drop: Ability to reorder bookmarks and folders directly on the board.
- Theme Engine: Support for 10+ built-in themes including Cyberpunk, Hacker, and Frosted Glass.
- Context Menu: Right-click functionality to add, rename, or delete bookmarks and folders.
- Quick Search: Real-time filtering of bookmarks with a dedicated search status indicator.
- Localization: Support for English, Japanese, and Chinese (Simplified/Traditional).
